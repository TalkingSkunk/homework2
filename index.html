About Sam Kim

As someone starting from the ground up, I am pursuing the mastery of front-end and back-end languages. I know I will never reach the finish line, as we are always pushing the boundaries with new technology, as we should.
I have learned a lot through Trilogy Fullstack Bootcamp program, and it often felt like drinking from a fire-hose. But steadily but surely, I have been able to form greater chunks of learning every day through diligent application of knowledge.

Motivated by my fascination with neuroscience, I have always striven to improve my communication skills through a Suicide Hotline, and Victim Services. I have practiced lateral thinking through a Patient Transportation Services, and learned to be versatile in skills utilization through working at Cineplex Cinemas.
